## Content Extracted:
Manas Microsystems Pvt Ltd. was established in the year 1998 and is engaged in the manufacturing of Various types of flow meters and other related process instruments.
The Compact Gas flow meter and Compact Steam flow meter is an indigenously developed product & has been launched in 2012. So far, the installations of this product have exceeded 2000 numbers at various places in India and other countries.
The Compact Gas Flow meter is applicable for measuring flow rates of Bio-gas, LPG, Compressed Air and other compressed gases in closed conduits and Compact Steam Flow meter is applicable for measuring flow rates of saturated and superheated steam, mass flow rates of boiler feed water in closed conduits. These flow meters are best suited for applications where accuracy, reliability and ruggedness are of prime concerns.
The Gas and Steam flow meter sizes available from DN15 line to DN200 line size (1/2" to 8").
The Compact Gas Flow meter is mostly used in various industries i.e. Automobile industry, Textile Industry, Steel Industry and Environmental industry. Compact Steam flow meter is used in Chemical / Pharmaceutical, Petrochemicals and Fertilizers sectors.
